How to make a NYC green guerilla grenade:

There are two types of seed grenade recipes (A and B):

Ingredients:

A.

  • Old glass Christmas ornaments
  • Small funnel
  • Pelletized, time-release fertilizer
  • Peatmoss "crumbs"
  • Tissue
  • Seeds

B.

  • Small balloons
  • Small funnel
  • Pelletized, time-release fertilizer
  • Peatmoss "crumbs"
  • Sink faucet
  • Seeds

Add seed and fertilizer to grenade membrane:

A.

Add seeds and fertilizer first, followed by moist peatmoss "crumbs". Stuff the opening at the top with a small piece of tissue. Gently shake to mix thoroughly.

B.

Add seeds and fertilizer first, followed by moistened peatmoss "crumbs". Stretch the mouth of the ballon over the faucet mouth and carefully fill with water. Tie off the opening. Gently shake to mix thoroughly.
